Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

JamesTobeht ("we", "our", or "the Platform") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our social media management platform.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to JamesTobeht, we access the following information through TikTok's official API:

  • Profile information: Your TikTok display name, avatar, and profile link
  • Account statistics: Follower count, following count, likes count, and video count
  • Video data: A list of your publicly published videos and their performance metrics
  • Publishing access: The ability to upload and publish video content to your profile on your behalf

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and maintain your session
  • Publish video content to your TikTok profile as authorized by you
  • Display your account analytics within the platform dashboard
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Retention

We store only the minimum data necessary to operate the Platform. Access tokens are stored securely and used only for authorized API calls. We do not retain video content after it has been published. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is never used for advertising purposes. We only share data with TikTok as required to operate the Platform through their official API.

6. TikTok API Data

Data accessed through TikTok's API is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy in addition to ours. We comply with all TikTok API Terms of Service and data handling requirements.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage of access tokens and secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke TikTok account access at any time through TikTok's settings

9. Children's Privacy

JamesTobeht is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at: james.tobeht@gmail.com
